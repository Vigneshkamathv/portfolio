PlantBud

In this project we have an automatic plant watering system with a companion website. It monitors soil moisture levels,water the plants and updates it on the website. User's can also set the timing's to water the plants.

Hard ware used- NodeMCU which is the brain of this system, it would communicate with the website and other component's . A 12v water pump and IC-L293D motor driver which helps in running the water pump . A water level indicator and other electronic components.

Language used - CSS, HTML, js, PHP, SQL and other additional languages.

Mobile signal booster

In this project we have an low cost mobile signal booster to address the common issue of weak mobile signals in certain areas by using 1800MHz frequency.

Hard ware used- IC-LM368 a low-power IC suitable for battery-powered applications. A crystal oscillator operating at 1800MHz frequency, commonly used in the 4G bands in India. Antennas other electronic components.
